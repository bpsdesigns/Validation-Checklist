FAQ

Here are some of our frequently asked questions:

What is the validation checklist?

An increasing frustration with the planning industry is the validation process. The validation checklist website is designed to help with this process by producing a standardised checklist document after answering questions about your application.

Who is this website for?

This website is for anyone in the UK planning industry that is preparing a submission to a local authority. After answering a series of questions, you are presented with a validation document requirement checklist for your submission.

Who owns this website website?

BPS Designs Limited owns and maintains the validation checklist website. BPS Designs is the company behind PlanMan. Planman is the leading online project management system for architects and town planners.
